# Get involved

Atomic Data is an open specification, and that means that you're very welcome to share your thougts and help make this standard as good as possible.
Drop an [issue on Github](https://github.com/ontola/atomic-data/issues), or join the [Discord server](https://discord.gg/ZspQv8) for voice / text chat.
